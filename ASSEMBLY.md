# Assembly Guide — No Soldering

1. 3D print frame (aether-frame.stl, matte black)
2. Snap Pi CM5 into temple slot
3. Plug LiDAR → Qwiic port 1
4. Plug LC lens driver → Qwiic port 2
5. Tape micro-LED behind waveguide lens
6. Hot-glue battery inside temple
7. Flash SD card with aether-standalone.img
8. Power on → say "Shades up"

Done. Truth loads in 2 seconds.
